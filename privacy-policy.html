Privacy Policy for PeakoTrack

Last updated: January 2026

PeakoTrack we respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

PeakoTrack does not collect personal information that directly identifies you, such as your name, email, phone number, or address.

We may collect the following types of data:

  • App usage data: general analytics about how the app is used.
  • Purchase data: subscription status and transactions, managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive your payment method or Apple ID.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, and crash logs, used only to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use non-sensitive and anonymized data solely to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Manage subscriptions through RevenueCat
  • Fix bugs and improve stability
  • Understand app performance

3. Third-Party Services

PeakoTrack uses third-party services to operate the app:

  • Apple App Store – handles all payments and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat – processes subscription status and receipts.

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

We do not store personal information on our servers. Subscription data is securely managed by Apple and RevenueCat.

5. Children’s Privacy

PeakoTrack is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of analytics data stored by third-party services. Contact us if needed.

How do I create a habit?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, choose Build or Quit, select an emoji, and set your habit details.

What's the difference between Build and Quit habits?

Build: Track habits you want to do regularly (exercise, reading). Requires daily check-ins.
Quit: Track habits you want to stop (smoking, junk food). Counts days since last occurrence.

How do I unlock Premium?

Tap the "+" button when you have 3 habits, or go to Profile ‚Üí "Upgrade to Premium".

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal information.

Can I sync across devices?

Not yet, but cloud sync is coming in a future update!

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Reset App Data". This will delete all habits from your device.
